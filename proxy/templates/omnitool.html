Earned SLA hours by team
Earned SLA+Additional hours by team:
  • Hours are only recognised in the month in which the WR is completed (status "Production Ready", "For Sign Off" or "Finished").
  • Each team is allocated a portion of a WR's earned value based on their proportion of the WR's timesheets. For example, a WR quoted at 20 hours that takes 10 hours of work by N7 and 5 hours assistance from SRE and has 5 hours of unused contingency will credit N7 with 10/15*20 hours and SRE with 5/15*20 hours.
  • For WRs with no quotes, time earned is just time spent by the members of each team.
  • This only includes "visible" time on SLA contracts, i.e. hours that show on the dashboard, and excludes Additional hours.

Earned revenue by person
Earned SLA+Additional revenue as hours:
  • Revenue is only recognised in the month in which the WR is completed (status "Production Ready", "For Sign Off" or "Finished").
  • Each person is allocated a portion of a WR's earned revenue based on their proportion of the WR's timesheets. For example, a WR quoted at 20 hours that takes 10 hours of dev and 6 hours of QA and has 4 hours of unused contingency will credit the dev with 10/14*20 hours of revenue and the tester with 4/14*20 hours.
  • For WRs with no quotes, time earned is just time spent by that person.
  • This only includes chargeable time on SLA contracts, i.e. hours that are visible on the dashboard, and does include Additional hours.
  • When thinking about an individual, also take into account work that's not captured here - documentation, mentoring, research, projects, etc.
